About

Maiyer Jadeyon Thao

Front-end Developer/Designer

Remember Myspace? It started long before Myspace, my love for web design and development that is. My dad bought my siblings and I, our very first computer when I was eight years old. A clunky, box-like Emachine that took snail's pace to start up while humming. From then, countless hours were spent playing with MS Paint and surfing the web.
Gurl Talk was the very first social platform that I partook in. Like Myspace, I had a customizable profile page with a guestbook, where I could add friends to my friend list. I learned how to code and designed themes for my profile. I became obsessed, crawled the internet, inspected others' codes, and hacked them into my own.
That passion transitioned into design school after high school. I studied graphic design and illustration with an emphasis on digital art at the Minneapolis College of Art and Design. I freelanced for many years doing web and graphic design projects. My thirst for knowledge for web development pushed me to go back to school. I graduated from the University of Minnesota in May 2018 with a full-stack development certificate. I am currently working as a front-end developer.
